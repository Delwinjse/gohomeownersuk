1. Introduction

Welcome to Go Homeowners UK ("we", "us", or "our"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you interact with our website https://gohomeownersuk.com or submit your details through Facebook Instant Forms as part of our ECO4 grant lead generation process.

2. Information We Collect

When you submit a form, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Confirmation of homeownership status
  • Confirmation that your boiler is over 15 years old
  • Confirmation that at least one person in your household receives means-tested benefits

3. How We Use Your Information

We collect your information solely to assess your eligibility for the ECO4 government grant scheme and to connect you with a certified installer who can assist you with a free home survey and installation services. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Evaluate ECO4 grant eligibility
  • Contact you regarding your application
  • Refer you to a qualified installer to carry out the necessary steps

4. Who We Share Your Data With

We share your information only with a single third-party company — a trusted and certified ECO4 installer. This installer is responsible for conducting surveys and completing installation work if you qualify. They do not resell or pass your data to any other parties and use it solely for the purpose of delivering ECO4 services.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent: You voluntarily provide your information through our form.
  • Legitimate Interest: To assess your eligibility and arrange necessary services related to the ECO4 scheme.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose it was collected for, typically no longer than 12 months, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Your Rights

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccuracies in your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: nottingclo@gmail.com

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, disclosure, or misuse.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
